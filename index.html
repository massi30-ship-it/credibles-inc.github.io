Credibles Inc. — Full Company Profile

About The Company

Credibles Inc. is a Lebanese cybersecurity artificial intelligence company founded on March 15, 2026 by Malik Hazem Assi, a 13 year old self taught developer and security researcher from Beirut, Lebanon. The company was born from a simple but powerful observation: cybersecurity is broken. Companies worldwide spend millions of dollars every year paying human bug bounty hunters to manually find vulnerabilities in their systems, a process that is slow, expensive, and inconsistent. Credibles Inc. exists to fix that permanently.

What We Build

Credibles Inc. is building an AI powered cybersecurity platform that automatically detects, analyzes, and fixes security vulnerabilities in websites, applications, and digital infrastructure without any human intervention. Think of it as a tireless, genius security researcher that works 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year, never takes a break, never misses a bug, and never sends an invoice for $50,000.

The Problem We Solve

Today, a company like Google, Microsoft, or Facebook pays individual security researchers anywhere from $500 to $250,000 per vulnerability found through platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd. Large enterprises spend between $50,000 and $500,000 per year on penetration testing alone. Small businesses and startups simply cannot afford this level of security, leaving them completely exposed to cyberattacks, data breaches, and financial devastation. Credibles Inc. changes everything. Our AI platform offers pricing plans ranging from $49 per month to $1000 per month, making world class cybersecurity accessible to businesses of different sizes, needs, and budgets.

What Our AI Does

  • Vulnerability Detection: Continuously scans websites, APIs, and applications for known and unknown security vulnerabilities including SQL injection, cross site scripting, path traversal, broken access control, authentication bypass, and hundreds more attack vectors.
  • Risk Assessment: Automatically classifies every vulnerability found by severity, critical, high, medium, or low, giving companies a clear picture of their security posture in real time.
  • Automated Fixing: Unlike traditional scanners that just report problems, Credibles AI actually patches and fixes vulnerabilities automatically, pushing secure code updates directly to the company's codebase.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Every time a company pushes new code or updates their systems, Credibles AI immediately rescans and ensures no new vulnerabilities were introduced.
  • Threat Intelligence: Connected to a global database of emerging threats, zero day vulnerabilities, and attack patterns, always learning, always updating, always one step ahead of hackers.
  • Compliance Reporting: Automatically generates detailed security reports for regulatory compliance including GDPR, ISO 27001, SOC 2, and more.

Market Opportunity

The global cybersecurity market is valued at $200 billion in 2026 and projected to reach $500 billion by 2030. Every single company with a website or digital product is a potential Credibles Inc. customer. There are over 400 million businesses worldwide. Capturing even a small share of that market creates major recurring revenue potential.

Competitive Advantage

Similar companies like Snyk, Veracode, and CrowdStrike exist, but none offer the complete combination of automated detection and automated fixing at an accessible price point for small and medium businesses. Credibles Inc. fills that gap.
